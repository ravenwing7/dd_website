The Stolen Lands: Geography

This is a wild land with few settlements. Those that exist are spartan and ill-equipped to deal with the harsh environment of the surrounding territory.

Beyond the civilized areas, the land is largely unexplored. Only the bravest, most adventurous spirits venture forth into the countryside. Untamed prairies are punctuated by wild rivers that feed into vast lakes. Thick, dark forests hide unfathomable secrets, and subterranean caves descend to unknowable depths.

This is the frontier of known lands. Great danger and grand adventure awaits around every turn and bend.
