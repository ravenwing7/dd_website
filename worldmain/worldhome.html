The Stolen Lands

Little is known of the region called The Stolen Lands. It is a wilderness fought over by noblemen and bandits, and by a natural realm still defending control of its territory. Civilized society is sparse and scattered, and danger lurks throughout the unknown reaches. But in lands unexplored, there is always high adventure and great treasure to be discovered. The land is a veritable fortune of fame and wealth, just waiting to be claimed by frontiersfolk. The very heart of an adventurer's life.
